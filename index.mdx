## Why NativeBridge?

NativeBridge is a cloud-powered platform that lets developers and teams launch, test, and interact with their mobile apps on real & virtual devices straight from the web.

Built to be fast, flexible, and intuitive, NativeBridge is the go-to solution for a variety of use cases, including:
